Post-operative Rehabilitation

We provide personalized recovery plans tailored to patients recovering from surgeries, such as joint replacements, spinal procedures, or heart surgeries. Our programs include targeted physical therapy, monitored progress through IoT devices, and innovative exercises using VR to speed up recovery and restore mobility.

Chronic Illness Management

Our chronic illness management programs help patients with conditions like diabetes, arthritis, and cardiovascular diseases. By combining wearable technology for real-time health monitoring and customized therapy plans, we aim to improve quality of life and reduce the burden of long-term health challenges.

Sports Injury Recovery

Whether you're a professional athlete or a fitness enthusiast, our center offers specialized programs for sports injury recovery. We focus on sports-specific rehabilitation, combining wearable tracking devices, advanced exercises, and recovery coaching to help you return to peak performance safely and quickly.

Preventive health and wellness for office workers and seniors

Designed for office workers, seniors, and those looking to maintain their health, our preventive programs focus on improving posture, reducing repetitive stress injuries, and enhancing mobility. These programs include ergonomic training, gentle exercises, and wellness workshops to prevent future health issues.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neurological rehabilitation services cater to patients recovering from strokes, spinal cord injuries, or other neurological conditions. Using VR/AR-based cognitive exercises, advanced physical therapy, and real-time progress tracking, we help patients regain strength, coordination, and confidence.

Pediatric Rehabilitation

Children recovering from injuries, surgeries, or developmental delays receive specialized care at our center. Our programs integrate fun, interactive therapies using AR/VR, age-appropriate exercises, and child-friendly monitoring devices to ensure effective and engaging rehabilitation.

Frequently Asked Questions

During your first visit, our specialists will conduct a comprehensive assessment of your condition, review your medical history, and develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and goals. The initial consultation typically takes 60-90 minutes.

The duration varies depending on your condition and goals. Post-operative rehabilitation typically ranges from 6-12 weeks, while sports injury recovery might take 4-8 weeks. Our team will provide a detailed timeline during your initial assessment.

  • VR/AR systems for immersive therapy
  • Smart wearables for progress tracking
  • Robotic assistance devices
  • AI-powered movement analysis
